The Taliban: An Overview


On August 15 2021, the Taliban seized Kabul, the capital of Afghanistan, and overall, the seat or right to govern the country, with the previous government leaving, surrendering, or retiring by the time the Taliban had re-entered Kabul. This major event in the country’s history has ignited both interest and concern from both locals and international voices alike. From wishes to make new alliances to condemnation from other nations, many of the world’s governments have to choose a side regarding the future of Afghanistan and its government.


With the nation entering a new phase in its history, a lot of questions may have been thought about this event regarding the country, the livelihoods of its citzens, and even with the international community, some of which have been answered and some of which that are yet to be. Nevertheless, this switch in government may change the country as a whole, for better or for worse.

A Brief Description Into the Subject:


From the hows to the whys, this website's main goal is to inform about the background and history of Afghanistan, from how the people live and have lived to the modern day history and events that lead to the known pre-Taliban Afghanistan. this website also aims to provide information regarding how such an organization could seize a country’s government, besides the nation as a whole, the beginnings of the Taliban and its history that lead to its essential government overthrow will also be discussed.


Asides from the events and histories that lead to this moment, the aftermath, such as the reactions, questions, and ways of moving forward will also be talked about. This will include the general public's reactions or comments from both Afghanistan and worldwide, as well as the given reactions from world leaders, such as thoughts or diplomatic responses, to those in-charge of important diplomatic ties with Afghanistan in general.
